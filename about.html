About Tiger Villa Zoo

Welcome to Tiger Villa Zoo! We love animals and want to share that love with you. Since 1984 , we've been working hard to create a special place for wildlife and for you.

Our Mission

At About Tiger Villa Zoo, we want to show you how amazing animals are. We're here to teach, inspire, and make sure you have a great time. Our goal is to help animals, educate you, and make sure our planet stays healthy for all living things.

Our Story

We started in 1984 with a small group of animals. Now, we've grown into a big zoo that cares about endangered species. Over the years, we've teamed up with others to help animals around the world.

Helping Wildlife

We don't just have fun with animals; we also work hard to keep them safe. We help save their homes, make sure endangered animals have babies, and teach everyone how important it is to protect nature.

Come Visit!

We want you to be part of the adventure! Whether you're here with your family, friends, or by yourself, there's something for everyone. Plan your visit and get ready for a day of fun and learning!
